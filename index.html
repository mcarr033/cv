About Me

My name is Manuel Carrillo and I graduated from the University of California, Riverside. I earned my bachelor's degree in United States History and my professional career has been in the accounting field. For the past 2 years, I have spent my free time learning game development in pursuit of my childhood dream of working in the gaming industry. In November 2022, I published my first asset for sale and have averaged $900 a month upon continued asset releases. I have also taken a web development course through Udemy which helped me learn how to design my own website and build off of bootstrap templates like this web page.
